Nurturing Faith Through Technology

This project was born out of my passion for clean, user-centered design and my desire to bring meaningful digital experiences to life. I’ve always found joy in creating things from scratch—whether it was sketching layouts in a notebook or experimenting with front-end code late into the night. When the opportunity came to build a portfolio project as part of the ALX Software Engineering program, I saw it as the perfect chance to showcase what I’ve learned and reflect my growth as a developer.

Over the past few months, I’ve been honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I knew I wanted to create something that felt authentic—something that truly represented me and my values as a creative, curious, and driven problem solver. I was inspired by modern web design trends, but also guided by a desire for simplicity, clarity, and elegance.

Our Mission: Everyday with Jesus

TreasuredLife was created to solve the challenge many churches face: maintaining a centralized online presence for access to sermons, devotionals, and announcements.

Our platform serves as a central hub for Christian teachings and relevant content, with an easy-to-update design that enables pastors and staff to add new materials without needing technical skills.

Whether you're seeking spiritual growth through sermons, daily devotionals, or church event updates, TreasuredLife provides the resources you need for your everyday walk with Jesus.
